Quick Answers

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered below:

How do I clear my recent calculations?

Your recent calculations are stored locally in your browser. You can clear them by clearing your browser's local storage or using your browser's privacy settings to clear site data.

Why do I get the same score every time?

Our algorithm is deterministic, meaning the same names and dates will always produce the same result. This ensures consistency and reliability.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions and feedback. Please email us with your ideas.

Is the app not working on my device?

The Love Calculator works on all modern browsers. If you're experiencing issues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. Include your browser and device details when contacting us.
